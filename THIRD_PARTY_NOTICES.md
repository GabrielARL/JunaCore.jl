## Radford Neal LDPC-codes helper programs

Files included at the repository root:

- `make-ldpc`
- `make-gen`
- `print-pchk`

These are Linux x86-64 builds of helper programs from Radford M. Neal's LDPC-codes package. `src/LDPC.jl` calls the copies under `tools/ldpc/` to construct and inspect LDPC parity-check/generator files.

Upstream:

- https://github.com/radfordneal/LDPC-codes
- https://glizen.com/radfordneal/ftp/LDPC-2012-02-11/index.html

The upstream Makefile says the programs are copyright Radford M. Neal and may be copied, used, modified, and distributed for any purpose if the copyright notice is retained, displayed prominently, and changes are noted. These bundled binaries are copied from the local scratch workspace without source changes.
